Certificate in Business Analytics (Certificate ) Starward Business School .

Business Analytics is an online certificate course that helps demystify data and strengthen learners’ analytical skills. Beginning with basic descriptive statistics and progressing to regression analysis, participants will implement analytical techniques in Excel and apply fundamental quantitative methods to real business problems.

Back End Web Development (Graduate Certificate) Division of Continuing Education.

Students pursuing a Graduate Certificate in Back-End Web Development will learn how to build and maintain the technology that powers the administrator side of a website. Through this professional graduate certificate, students gain vital knowledge and skills to drive communication between technology platforms and to power the back-end of a website.

Business Communication (Graduate Certificate) Division of Continuing Education.

Students pursuing a Graduate Certificate in Business Communication will learn to deliver clear, concise, and convincing messaging to advance ideas, build consensus, and resolve conflict.
